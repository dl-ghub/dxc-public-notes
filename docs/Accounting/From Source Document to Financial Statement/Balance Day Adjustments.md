## Examples of BDAs
- Unearned Income (Income received in advance)
- Accrued Income (Income has yet to be received)
- Prepaid Expenses (Expense paid in advance)
- Accrued Expenses (Expense has yet to be paid)
- Depreciation
- Allowance for Doubtful Debts (eestimate of how much AR that may not be able to collect)
